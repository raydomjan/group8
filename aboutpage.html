About our Webpage

We discovered that most first time home buyers are freshly graduated college students who don't have much capital, want a cheap house and preferably want a house that does not have a downpayment. Usually these houses are the ones that are foreclosed. This website is going to give first time home buyers a place to:

  • Look at the areas in form of a heatmap that have foreclosed houses
  • Analyze the area based on the foreclosures.
  • Finally, will be able to make decisions based on the information provided
